PG

To develop point guard (PG) skills in basketball, you need to focus on several key areas that define this position. Here's a breakdown of important skills for a successful point guard: 1. Ball Handling
Master advanced dribbling techniques to maintain control under pressure.
Practice changing speeds and directions to break defenders.
2. Passing
Develop precision passing, including bounce, chest, and no-look passes.
Focus on making quick decisions to assist teammates in scoring.
3. Court Vision and Awareness
Enhance your ability to see the entire court and anticipate plays.
Recognize defensive setups and find open teammates.
